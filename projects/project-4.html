Problem Statement

Needed a portfolio that effectively demonstrates both design and development skills while being fast, accessible, and easy to maintain. Most portfolio templates were either too generic or too complex.

Research & Approach

Analyzed 20+ developer portfolios and conducted A/B testing with 30 recruiters. Found that clean, minimal designs with clear project stories performed 40% better in initial screening.

Design & Figma

Designed a whitespace-driven layout in Figma with focus on typography and project storytelling. Created a consistent visual system with custom CSS properties and smooth micro-interactions.
